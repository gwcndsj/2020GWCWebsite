Melissa de Waal

Freshman

The game that I created is a maze-based game where you are a cat, and your owner has left you alone in his house for 100 seconds. Your goal is to destroy everything you can before your owner gets back, and if you destroy everything within the time limit then you win. Something challenging about creating the game was that I wasn't sure how to make it so the cat couldn't go through the maze walls, because before it could just walk through the lines I drew as if they weren't even there. Some interesting things about my game is that each item has a different animation when the cat touches it, which I think gives it a more realistic look instead of each object just disappearing like the game was originally designed. For example, clothing items become shredded and ripped, and a fishbowl falls off the table and spills water. Thanks for checking my game out!
